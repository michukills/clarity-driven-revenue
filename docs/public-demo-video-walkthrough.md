## Narration script (3–5 minutes)

**Open.**
> Most small business problems do not start as one big failure. Usually, one gear starts slipping. Follow-up gets inconsistent. The owner becomes the approval point for everything. Financial visibility gets fuzzy. The RGS OS is built to find those slipping gears, organize the evidence, and turn the findings into a repair map the owner can actually use.

**Scorecard.**
> It starts with the 0–1000 Business Stability Scorecard. It is self-reported and preliminary. It points attention. It does not replace the paid Diagnostic.

**Diagnostic path.**
> If the owner wants a deeper look, the Diagnostic is a paid inspection of the business. It is not a guaranteed outcome. It is an honest examination of where the system is slipping.

**Portal welcome.**
> Once a client is in the portal, the first thing they see is a guided welcome. The point is for the owner to always know what the next step is.

**Owner Diagnostic Interview.**
> The first required step is the Owner Diagnostic Interview. The owner explains the business in their own words before any tool runs. Context first.

**Tool sequence.**
> From there, the system assigns diagnostic tools based on industry and stage. A new owner does not get the same tools as an established operator.

**Admin review.**
> Every diagnostic gets reviewed by an admin. The Industry Brain provides context and emphasis based on the industry. Nothing is auto-published to the client.

**Score.**
> The 0–1000 Business Stability Score is deterministic. The same answers always produce the same score. Industry emphasis changes how the score is interpreted. It does not change the base score.

**Report.**
> The report is built in a draft state, reviewed, and then released to the client. Internal admin notes never appear in the client view.

**Stability Snapshot.**
> The Stability Snapshot summarizes strengths, risks, and opportunities at a glance.

**Priority Repair Map.**
> The Priority Repair Map tells the owner what to fix first and why. Prioritization is the point. Not a long, undifferentiated list.

**Implementation.**
> Implementation installs the repair plan. The Implementation Roadmap, SOP and Training Bible, Decision Rights and Accountability, Workflow and Process Mapping, and the Tool Assignment and Training Tracker work together. This is the repair pass. It is not unlimited support.

**RGS Control System.**
> Once the work is done, the RGS Control System keeps important signals visible. The Revenue Control System lives inside it, alongside the Revenue and Risk Monitor, the Priority Action Tracker, the Owner Decision Dashboard, the Scorecard History, and the Monthly System Review.

**Industries.**
> RGS supports general and mixed business, trades and field service, restaurant and food service, retail, and cannabis dispensary operations — including MMJ, medical marijuana, and recreational. Cannabis support is operational visibility only. It is not legal advice and not a compliance certification.

**Demo safety.**
> Everything you are seeing uses sample data. No real client data. No fake results. No guaranteed outcomes.

**Close.**
> The point is not to make the owner need RGS forever. The point is to make the business clearer. The Diagnostic finds the slipping gears. Implementation installs the repair plan. The RGS Control System keeps the owner connected to the system without turning RGS into an operator inside the business.
>
> If the same problems keep coming back, check the system. See how stable your business really is — start with the 0–1000 Business Stability Scorecard.
