# 0-1000 Business Stability Scorecard Walkthrough

Shows how to answer the scorecard as a starting signal across the five RGS gears.

Demo account / sample data. Internal logic protected.

## Scorecard

Answer for the last 60 to 90 days, using the normal recent pattern.

## Five gears

The scorecard looks across demand, conversion, operations, financial visibility, and owner independence.

## Evidence

Choose the closest honest answer. Uncertainty is useful visibility.

## Result

A 0-1000 score helps locate where the system may be slipping, but final findings require RGS review.

## Next

Use the result as a starting point for what to inspect next, not as a final diagnosis.
