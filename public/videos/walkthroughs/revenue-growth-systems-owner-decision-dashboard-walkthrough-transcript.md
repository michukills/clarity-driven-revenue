# Owner Decision Dashboard Walkthrough

Shows how owner-level decisions are kept visible without replacing owner judgment.

Demo account / sample data. Internal logic protected.

## Decision support

Start with what needs owner review. The dashboard is meant to reduce scattered decision-making.

## Source

Notice where each item came from: a priority action, risk signal, scorecard, roadmap, or review.

## Owner question

Read the decision question and the recommended owner review before deciding what to do next.

## Review date

Check the due date or next review date so the decision does not disappear.

## Boundary

This dashboard supports owner review. It does not replace your judgment or final authority.
