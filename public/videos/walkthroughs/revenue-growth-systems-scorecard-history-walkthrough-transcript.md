# Scorecard History Walkthrough

Shows how score history is used as a trend signal without overclaiming precision.

Demo account / sample data. Internal logic protected.

## Trend

Start with the stability trend. One score is a snapshot; history shows movement.

## Gears

Look across the five gears. Movement in one gear can put pressure on another.

## Context

Ask what changed before overreading the movement.

## Review

Use the trend to focus the next review and choose what should be inspected next.

## Boundary

The score is a visibility signal, not a guarantee of results or perfect data.
