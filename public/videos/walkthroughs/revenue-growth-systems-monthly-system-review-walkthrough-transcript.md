# Monthly System Review Walkthrough

Shows how to use the monthly review rhythm as a bounded visibility cadence.

Demo account / sample data. Internal logic protected.

## Monthly rhythm

Start with what changed this month and what needs review next.

## Signals

Review priorities, risk signals, and score movement together so the month tells one operating story.

## Owner input

Add what RGS should know: context, blockers, and anything that changed since the last review.

## Next focus

Leave the review with one clear next focus and a practical review cadence.

## Boundary

This is scheduled visibility and interpretation, not emergency support or unlimited advisory access.
