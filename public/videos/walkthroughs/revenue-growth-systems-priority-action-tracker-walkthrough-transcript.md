# Priority Action Tracker Walkthrough

Shows how to read the small number of actions RGS has marked as important now.

Demo account / sample data. Internal logic protected.

## Priority

Start with what is due this week. The tracker keeps the next few actions visible.

## Why it matters

Read why the action matters before changing anything in the business.

## Owner

Check who owns the next move. RGS can guide review, but final authority stays with the owner.

## Signal

Look for the success signal, then review it again on the next operating cadence.

## Boundary

This is a bounded priority tracker, not a full task-management suite or emergency support system.
